I never believed in aliens. Not until last week...I live on a space-ship. A great big space-ark. My father is the Captain. We travel through deep space, making star maps.

I was in the school-room with my gang, Roa and Wokp. Just then there was a fizz and a thud. They came from the landing bay. We rushed out. There sat a little shuttle, bashed and dented. And out of the shuttle climbed... the alien.

The alien took off its space-suit. We froze in fear. It took off its helmet. We gasped in horror. For a start, where was all its mucus? It hardly had a strange fur only on its head and was a weird brown colour. Fur should be purple, red or blue, like ours!

The alien's face was a horrible beige. And it had two eyes! Surely one is quite enough for anybody. It had no proper tentacles at all - just four long sticks, with five pink wigglies at each end.
